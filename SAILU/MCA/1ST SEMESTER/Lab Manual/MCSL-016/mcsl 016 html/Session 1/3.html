HTML(Hypertext Markup Language)

Basics of HTML

Web pages or material in form of hypermedia documents accessed through the internet can be located anywhere in the world.

Html is used to define documents structured and format with the help of single tag or a pair of tags.
A tag is a string in the language surrounded by less than (<) and a greater than (>) sign.
An opening tag does not being with a slash (/).
An ending or closing tag is a string that being with a slash(/) .
